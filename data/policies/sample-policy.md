# Sample Company Policy

## Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to the following types of leave:

### Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of annual leave per year
- Annual leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance
- Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next year

### Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Sick leave can be taken with a medical certificate for absences longer than 3 days
- Sick leave does not require advance notice but should be reported as soon as possible

### Casual Leave
- Employees receive 5 days of casual leave per year
- Casual leave can be used for personal matters and does not require advance notice
- Casual leave cannot be carried forward

## Attendance Policy

### Check-in and Checkout
- All employees must check in when arriving at the office
- All employees must check out when leaving the office
- Check-in and checkout times are recorded for attendance tracking
- Late arrivals should be communicated to the manager

### Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible working hours are available with manager approval
- Overtime work must be pre-approved by the department head

## Remote Work Policy

- Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval
- Employees must be available during core business hours (10:00 AM - 4:00 PM)
- Remote work requests should be submitted at least 1 week in advance
